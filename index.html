Santa Fe

Santa Fe is a province located in the east-central region of Argentina. The city of Santa Fe also serves as the capital of the province. This region experiences a warm, highly humid climate and holds the distinction of being the second most important province in Argentina, as well as the most populous after Buenos Aires.Its significance stems from its robust agricultural sector, contributing substantially to Argentina's grain and livestock production. Furthermore, Santa Fe's strategic location along major transportation routes facilitates trade and commerce, bolstering its economic influence.
